×Hello! Welcome to my Yoga website

I first began my interest in yoga and movement whilst I was training to be an actor. For me yoga started as a way of waking up, warming up, and getting my body, brain and breath ready to rehearse. Before I knew it I was practicing every day and loving it - so I thought it was about time I trained and started teaching too!

I trained in South Goa, India, and have returned there for an internship to further my training and practice.

I love yoga because is accessible to all and it is a practical way to see how we each develop in our own special ways. Some people will be awesome at backbends, others rock at arm balances whilst some are wizards of the mind and meditation. It's like everyone’s their own special kind of superhero, you just need to take the time to see it – which kind are you?

  • I teach a number of regular weekly classes around London and am also available for one-to-one private sessios, group classes, corporate hire.
  • Available to teach Vinyasa Flow, Ashtanga, Hatha, Core Power flow.
  • Fully accredited and insured by Yoga Alliance UK.
